    What is it?

    About The Event

    Shark Tank is a high-energy entrepreneurship competition where students pitch their startup ideas to a panel of "sharks" — judges, investors, and industry experts.

    Pitch Your Idea

    Present your innovative business idea to a panel of industry-leading judges and mentors.

    Network & Connect

    Meet like-minded entrepreneurs, investors, and mentors who can help shape your vision.

    Stage Presence

    Hone your public speaking and presentation skills in front of a live audience.

    Win Prizes

    Top pitches win exciting cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, and incubation support.
